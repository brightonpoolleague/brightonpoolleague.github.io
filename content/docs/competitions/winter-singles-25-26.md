# Winter Singles 2025/26

## Prize Pot

- Total: £640
- Winner: £320 + trophy
- Runner-up: £160 + trophy 
- Semi-Finalists: £80

## Format

- Races to 5 all the way through
- International rules
- Randomly drawn each round
- 3 week windows to arrange and play your matches
- If drawn as the home player, you must play from a Brighton league venue
- Final to be played on presentation night (30th March 2026)
